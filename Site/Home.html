Number Shapes is a visual language for arithmetic that gives students the opportunity to advance their understanding of numbers before they are forced to master raw computation. Too often students begin their study of mathematics by learning "tricks" for manipulating arbitrary symbols. Number shapes creates an environment where students can practice problem solving instead of memorizing and repeating procedures. - Andrew Fenner (creator)
